The Giza Pyramid Complex is an archaeological site on the Giza Plateau, on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt. It includes the three great pyramids, the Great Sphinx, several tombs, a workers' village and an industrial complex. It is located in the Western Desert, about 9 km (5.6 mi) west of the Nile River in the Old Town of Giza, and about 13 km (8.3 mi) southwest of downtown Cairo.
